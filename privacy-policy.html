Last updated: June 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how একটি মালিক অটো ফিলার ("the Extension") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Chrome Extension. This Extension is designed to assist users of undp.ldtax.gov.bd by automatically filling in land owner details during the registration process.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Extension does not sell, share, or misuse your personal data in any way.

1. Information We Collect

The Extension collects only the minimum information necessary to function:

  • Name — entered by the user during registration for access approval
  • Mobile number — used as a unique identifier for approval status lookup
  • Land owner data — name, father's name, and address entered by the user to enable auto-fill functionality

We do not collect passwords, payment information, national ID numbers, browsing history, or any data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Your name and mobile number are stored in Firebase Realtime Database solely to verify whether you have been granted access by the administrator.
  • Your land owner data (owner name, father's name, address) is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local and is never transmitted to any server.
  • No data is used for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Extension.

3. Data Storage

  • Local storage (on your device): Land owner auto-fill data is stored only on your device via chrome.storage.local. It never leaves your device.
  • Remote storage (Firebase Realtime Database): Your name, mobile number, and approval status are stored in a Firebase Realtime Database located in the asia-southeast1 (Singapore) region, operated by Google LLC under their infrastructure.
Firebase's data handling is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third party, advertiser, analytics provider, or external service — except for Firebase Realtime Database (Google), which is used solely for storing approval status as described above.

The Extension does not communicate with any server other than undp.ldtax.gov.bd (the target government portal) and Firebase Realtime Database.

5. Permissions Justification

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — to save land owner data and approval status locally on your device
  • tabs — to detect when you are on undp.ldtax.gov.bd and send auto-fill data to the active tab
  • scripting — to inject auto-fill functionality into undp.ldtax.gov.bd pages
  • host_permissions (undp.ldtax.gov.bd) — to run the auto-fill content script on the target government portal only
  • host_permissions (*.firebasedatabase.app) — to check approval status from Firebase Realtime Database

No permission is requested beyond what is strictly necessary for the Extension to function.

6. User Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access — contact us to know what data we hold about you
  • Delete — request deletion of your name and mobile number from our database at any time
  • Remove local data — uninstalling the Extension automatically removes all locally stored data from your device

To request data deletion, contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

This Extension is intended for professional use by government land tax officials and authorized personnel only. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
